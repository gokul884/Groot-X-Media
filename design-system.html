Color strategy

Restrained with a strong accent. ~60% neutral/white surfaces, ~30% navy + ink text and structure, ~10% orange for action. Navy earns bigger blocks in two deliberate places only: the "Most Popular" pricing plan and the contact info panel. Orange never carries large fills except buttons.

Never use pure #000 or #fff for text/backgrounds (logo SVG strokes excepted). No gradient text. No side-stripe accent borders.

4. Spacing & layout

  • Base grid: 4pt system (4, 8, 12, 16, 22, 26, 32, 44, 60, 80px).
  • Container: .wrap, max-width 1160px, 28px side padding.
  • Section rhythm: 70–80px vertical padding, varied intentionally.
  • Grids: 3-up, collapsing to 2-up at 900px and 1-up at 600px.
  • Radius: cards 20–26px, pills/buttons 999px, icon tiles 11–13px.

5. Components

  • Buttons: .btn-primary (orange, glow), .btn-ghost (bordered, orange on hover), .btn-white (on navy). Pill-shaped, lift 2px on hover.
  • Cards: white surface, 1px line border, hover lift + shadow, growing corner-blob (offers) or image zoom (blog). Never nested.
  • Nav: sticky, blurred translucent, gains border + shadow on scroll (.scrolled). Animated orange underline; active link orange. Five links: Home · Services · Pricing · Blog · Contact.
  • Footer: light navy-tinted, 5-column grid, social pills fill orange on hover, ambient blob behind.
  • Forms: rounded inputs on --card-2, orange focus ring. Inline success state, no modals.
  • FAQ: native <details>, orange plus-icon rotating to a cross when open.
  • Icons: Lucide, inline, inheriting color.

6. Motion

  • Easing: exponential ease-out. --ease: cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1). No bounce/elastic.
  • Scroll reveal: .reveal fades + rises 26px via IntersectionObserver (threshold 0.12).
  • Stagger: .stagger children sequence at --i * 70ms.
  • Ambient float: blobs drift on a 14s alternating loop with offset delays.
  • Hover: cards lift, images scale 1.06, arrows nudge right.
  • Blog post: top reading-progress bar + sticky share rail.
  • Only transform and opacity animate. Reveals 600–700ms, micro 100–250ms.

9. Responsive

  • 900px: grids collapse, hero media stacks above copy, desktop nav hides (logo + CTA remain), footer 2-column.

  • Tap targets ≥44px throughout.

10. Do / Don't

Do

  • Keep orange rare, reserved for action.
  • Reuse the SVG logo symbol, never retype the wordmark.
  • Tint any new neutral toward hue 262.
  • Add scroll-reveal to new sections.

Don't

  • Introduce a second accent color.
  • Use gradient text, glassmorphism, or side-stripe borders.
  • Nest cards inside cards.
  • Animate layout properties. Use transform + opacity.
  • Use em dashes in copy.
